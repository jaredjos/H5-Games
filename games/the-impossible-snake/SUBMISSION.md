# Submission Metadata

## Title

The Impossible Snake

## Short Description

Guide a biomechanical serpent through ten escalating 3D arenas filled with traps, hunters, and volatile power-ups.

## Full Description

The Impossible Snake transforms the classic arcade game into a cinematic ten-level survival run. Collect every energy core, adapt to shifting obstacles, outmaneuver hunters, and use Aegis, Phase, and Fang boosters to turn danger into an advantage. Configure the controls and number of lives, then chase a higher score through increasingly hostile arenas.

## Controls

- Desktop: WASD or Arrow keys to steer; Space to pause or resume.
- Mobile and tablet: swipe anywhere on the arena or use the on-screen direction pad.
- Menus: mouse, touch, or keyboard focus controls.

## Display

- Supported orientation: landscape and portrait.

## Monetization

- Midgame placement: natural pause-to-resume transition.
- CrazyGames-only additional midgame placement: retry or restart after a loss.
- Rewarded placement: one optional out-of-lives revive per run.
- The game always provides a non-ad restart path.

## Data and Privacy

- No account is required.
- No third-party analytics or tracking requests.
- Player name, options, high score, and progress are stored locally.
- Platform SDKs provide portal analytics and advertising only in their respective builds.
